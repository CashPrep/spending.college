Methodology
How the estimate works

Three data layers combine to give you a location-accurate monthly figure.

01

National Baseline

We start from the $2,400–$2,600/yr national average for student "other expenses" per College Board Trends in College Pricing data.

02

State Cost Index

The baseline is scaled by your state's 2025 cost-of-living index sourced from the MERIC/C2ER Cost of Living Data Series — from Oklahoma (85) to Hawaii (184).

03

Lifestyle Adjustments

Housing type, meal plan, transport, entertainment, and wellness modifiers are layered on top for your personal total.

About tap to read

College Job Hours pairs with College Cash. It compares your yearly spending target to what you might earn from a job. It is a planning tool only, not a promise of any specific job, wage, or income.

In “I already have a job” mode, yearly earnings are calculated as hourly pay × hours per week × paid weeks. In “Help me plan a job” mode, suggested hours assume about 32 working weeks per year (roughly two semesters).

Many students work part‑time during the school year and more during breaks, but the right schedule depends on your classes, health, and local job options.

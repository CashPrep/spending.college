About the data tap to expand

College Job Hours is designed to pair with College Cash. It takes your monthly spending target and turns it into yearly dollars, then compares that to what you’d earn at a given hourly wage, hours per week, and number of paid weeks. [web:23][web:27]

The math is straightforward: yearly earnings = wage × hours per week × paid weeks. Yearly target = monthly spending × 12. This shows whether you cover your target or fall short, and by how much. [web:23]

Typical student jobs pay anywhere from minimum wage to well above it, and weekly hours vary a lot by campus and semester. Use this as a planning tool, not a guarantee that you’ll get a specific job or schedule. [web:24][web:27]

This is not financial advice. It’s a simple calculator to help you think about how work hours and pay line up with your spending goals.

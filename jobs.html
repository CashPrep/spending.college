    About tap to read

    College Job Hours pairs with College Cash. It compares your yearly spending target to what you might earn from a job. It is a planning tool only — not a guarantee of any specific income.

    In "I already have a job" mode, yearly earnings are calculated as hourly pay × hours per week × paid weeks. In "Help me plan" mode, suggestions assume about 32 working weeks per year.
